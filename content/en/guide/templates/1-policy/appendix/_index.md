## Appendix 1. Roster of Responsible Personnel

| No | Role | Responsibility | Required competencies | Owning organization | Owner |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Open Source Program Manager (OSPM) | Holds overall responsibility for the company's open source program. | Understanding of the software development process<br>Understanding of copyright and patents<br>Expert knowledge of open source license compliance<br>Communication skills | Open Source Management Team | [Name] |
| 2 | Legal | Assesses legal risks related to open source licenses and provides legal counsel. | Understanding of the open source ecosystem<br>Expert knowledge of software copyright<br>Expert knowledge of open source licenses<br>Ability to assess legal risk | Legal Team | [Name] |
| 3 | IT | Operates and automates open source analysis tools. | Understanding of the open source license compliance process<br>Understanding of open source analysis tools<br>Expert knowledge of IT infrastructure<br>Understanding of automation and CI/CD pipelines | IT Team | [Name] |
| 4 | Security | Operates open source security vulnerability analysis tools. | Understanding of DevSecOps<br>Understanding of open source security vulnerability analysis tools<br>Expert knowledge of known vulnerabilities and newly discovered vulnerabilities<br>Ability to assess and manage risk | Security Team | [Name] |

| 6 | Quality | Verifies open source license obligations when distributing Supplied Software. | Understanding of the software development process<br>Basic knowledge of open source license compliance<br>Understanding of the open source policy<br>Basic knowledge of open source licenses | Quality Assurance Team | [Name] |
